Hello! Welcome to page about baseball!

This page will be about baseball.

  • What is the sport about? This sport is played all around the world. Baseball is a bats and balls game. You need a team of nine players on each side. The game time is approximately around three hours for a nine inning game.

  • What education do you need? In order to be a baseball player, you don't need a degree. You just need to have gone through highschool, and/or college.

  • What is the average age? If you want to play in the Major Leagues, the age group ranges from about 19-35. Majority of the professional players play from about 20 years old to about 35 years old.

  • How long do they play for? If they really have a passion for the sport, the play until age limits them. The average player plays for probably 8-14 seasons.
